INSTRUCTIONS

As soon as the light turns green, click the space bar. Whoever clicks the spacebar first wins the round!

If you win the round, you have three options:

  1. Save Your Tokens. Remember, your tokens will increase in value each round

  2. Convert a number of tokens to points

  3. Deduct a number of points from your opponent

Whoever has the most points after 30 rounds wins the game!
